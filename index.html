CAPABILITIES

Composite Printing Services

01

Continuous Carbon Fiber

Continuous carbon fiber (CCF) reinforcement in nylon and PETG matrix. Tensile strength up to 1400 MPa — competitive with aluminum at 60% of the weight. Fiber routing optimized to load path for maximum structural efficiency.

02

Kevlar & Fiberglass Reinforcement

Continuous Kevlar for impact resistance and toughness in tool housings and protective components. Continuous fiberglass for cost-effective structural reinforcement where CF is overspecified.

03

Chopped Fiber & FFF Hybrid

Chopped carbon and glass fiber FFF for complex geometries where continuous fiber routing is not possible. 35% CF-Nylon and 20% GF-PETG for structural chassis, brackets, and housings with improved stiffness.

COMPOSITE MATERIALS

Fiber Reinforcement Options

The fiber reinforcement defines structural performance. Our material portfolio covers the full range from cost-effective glass fiber to aerospace-grade carbon.

  • ● Continuous Kevlar — superior impact resistance
  • ● Continuous fiberglass — cost-effective structural reinforcement
  • ● 35% chopped carbon nylon — complex geometry parts
  • ● 20% glass-filled PETG — production-grade stiffness
  • ● High-temperature composites: PEEK-CF and PEKK-CF
